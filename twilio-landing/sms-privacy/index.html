Privacy Policy — Chatforia

Last updated: 2026-02-23

What we collect

We may collect your phone number, device identifiers, and minimal account information required to provide messaging services.

SMS & messaging

If you provide your mobile phone number and consent, you agree to receive SMS messages from Chatforia for account and conversation-related purposes. Typical messages include login alerts, verification codes, message delivery alerts, and security notices. Message frequency varies. Msg & data rates may apply.

Data sharing and marketing

Chatforia does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes. We will not provide end-user data to third parties for marketing without explicit user consent.

We may share limited personal data (for example, phone numbers or routing metadata) with service providers and subprocessors — such as messaging carriers, hosting providers, and analytics services — solely as necessary to operate and deliver the Chatforia service (for example, to send verification or account-related SMS). Any such sharing is limited to the minimum information necessary and is governed by contracts that prohibit use for marketing or promotional purposes.

Opt-out

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any Chatforia SMS. To resubscribe reply START. For help reply HELP or email support@chatforia.com.
