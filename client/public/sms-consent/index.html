Chatforia SMS Notification Consent

Chatforia sends SMS notifications related to your account activity and messaging events.

Types of messages

  • Verification codes
  • Login alerts and security notifications
  • New message notifications and message delivery notices

Message purpose

All messages are transactional or conversational and relate to account security and in-app messaging. Chatforia does not send promotional or marketing messages from this number.

Message frequency

Message frequency varies based on user activity.

Charges

Message and data rates may apply.

Opt-out

You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

Help

Reply HELP for assistance or contact support at support@chatforia.com.

Consent

By providing your phone number during account registration and confirming verification, you agree to receive transactional SMS notifications from Chatforia.
